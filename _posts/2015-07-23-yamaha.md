Initially a 2003 Yamaha TW200, my dad and I made some modifications:

- Deleted airbox for cone filters
- Fabricated mudplate & welded support tabs
- Powder coated frame + other parts
- Painted tank
- Upgraded jets
- Aftermarket parts
		- Blinkers
    - Headlight
    - Tailight
    - Exhaust
    - Seat
    - Front fender
    - Battery box
